AE Tax Advisors in the News: Recent Press & Coverage

AE Tax Advisors · Tax Planning & Strategy

As a boutique tax advisory firm, we don't pursue broad media coverage. Instead, we focus on speaking to high-income professionals through targeted channels. Here's where you can find AE Tax Advisors:

Tax Planning Publications & Interviews

Our team contributes to professional tax and financial planning publications. We've been quoted or featured in discussions about: Cost segregation strategies for real estate investors (CPA Practice Advisor, 2024), S-corp taxation and self-employment tax minimization (The CPA Journal, 2024), Tax planning for high-income professionals (Financial Planning Magazine, 2023-2024), Audit defense and IRS representation (Professional Tax Publications, 2024).

These articles focus on practical tax planning strategies, not marketing AE Tax Advisors specifically. We believe the best marketing is delivering excellent service and helping clients share their results through case studies.

Speaking Engagements

Our team has presented at: National Association of Real Estate Investment Fiduciaries (tax strategy for real estate investors), Small Business Owner Association (entity selection and tax planning for entrepreneurs), CPA Continuing Education Conferences (advanced tax strategies and audit defense), Professional Associations (physicians, attorneys, consultants on tax optimization).

If your professional organization is interested in having us speak about tax planning tailored to your industry, contact us at team@aetaxadvisors.com.

Case Studies and Client Results

We regularly publish case studies showing real results for clients (with names and identifying details removed). Recent examples include: A real estate investor who implemented cost segregation and reduced taxable income by $120,000 in year one. A business owner who restructured as an S-corp and saved $35,000 annually in self-employment taxes. A physician who used a backdoor Roth strategy and accumulated $500,000+ in tax-free retirement savings over 10 years. A business seller who used strategic deductions and entity planning to reduce capital gains tax by $150,000.

These aren't hypothetical examples. They're real clients achieving real results. If you're curious whether we could achieve similar results for you, schedule a discovery call.

Industry Recognition

Our team members hold professional designations and credentials: Certified Public Accountants (CPA), Enrolled Agents (IRS-credentialed tax specialists), Members of the National Association of Enrolled Agents, Continuing education in Advanced Tax Strategies, Cost Segregation, Real Estate Tax Planning.

While we don't pursue awards or rankings, we maintain these credentials to ensure we're held to the highest professional standards.

Research and Thought Leadership

We publish occasional white papers and research on tax planning strategies. Recent pieces include: "Cost Segregation for Real Estate Investors: A Comprehensive Guide" (white paper, 2024), "Tax Planning for Business Owners: Entity Selection, Self-Employment Tax, and Exit Strategies" (guide), "Opportunity Zones and Capital Gains Planning: Strategies for High-Net-Worth Investors" (analysis).

These are available to prospects and clients. They demonstrate our depth of knowledge and commitment to client education.

Why We Don't Pursue Broad Press Coverage

Most tax advisory firms pursue media attention as a marketing tactic. They're quoted in mainstream media about general tax advice. While this builds brand awareness, it's not how high-income professionals find tax advisors. Instead, they rely on referrals, professional networks, and direct recommendations.

We focus on being known within our niche: high-income professionals, business owners, real estate investors. This targeted approach attracts ideal clients.

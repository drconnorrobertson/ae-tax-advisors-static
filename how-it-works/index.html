How It Works

From your first call to ongoing advisory -- here is exactly how AE Tax Advisors builds and implements your custom tax strategy.

Your Path to Tax Savings

A proven 6-step process that has helped hundreds of business owners and real estate investors reduce their tax burden.

1

Free Discovery Call

30-minute confidential call to understand your current tax situation, business structure, income sources, and goals. We identify the biggest opportunities and determine whether our advisory services are a fit. No obligation, no pressure -- just a straightforward conversation about your taxes.

2

Document Collection

We request your last 3 years of tax returns, K-1s, W-2s, 1099s, closing statements, and any relevant financial documents. Our secure client portal makes uploading easy. We handle all the organization -- you just send us what you have.

3

3-Year Lookback Analysis

Our team conducts a comprehensive review of your last 3 years of filed returns. We identify every missed deduction, overlooked credit, improper classification, and optimization opportunity. This analysis typically uncovers $50,000 to $500,000+ in potential savings from prior years alone.

4

Custom Tax Plan

We build your personalized tax optimization strategy with exact dollar savings for each recommendation. Your plan covers entity structuring, retirement plan design, cost segregation, accountable plans, Augusta Rule, income timing, and every applicable IRC-compliant strategy. Every recommendation includes the specific IRC section and implementation steps.

5

Implementation

We handle all the paperwork. Amended returns, entity elections, S-Corp filings, cost segregation studies, retirement plan establishment, bookkeeping cleanup -- whatever your plan calls for, we execute it. You approve, we implement. No loose ends.

6

Ongoing Advisory

Quarterly check-ins to review your financial position and adjust strategy. Mid-year tax projections to avoid surprises. Year-end planning sessions to lock in deductions before December 31. Your dedicated advisor knows your situation and is available when you need them. Our goal: keep your tax bill as close to $0 as legally possible.

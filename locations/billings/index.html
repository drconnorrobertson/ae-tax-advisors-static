Why Billings Businesses Choose AE Tax Advisors

Montana has no state sales tax, which makes it attractive for business operations, but that does not mean tax planning is simple. Business owners in Billings face federal tax obligations, payroll tax decisions, entity structuring questions, and complex depreciation rules that require expert guidance. The difference between a reactive tax preparer and a proactive tax advisor can amount to tens of thousands of dollars in annual savings.

AE Tax Advisors was built on one principle: every client deserves a tax strategy, not just a tax return. When you work with our Billings office, you get a dedicated team that reviews your financials, identifies overlooked deductions, restructures your entities for maximum efficiency, and builds a multi-year plan that compounds savings over time.

Our expertise in cost segregation is particularly valuable for Montana real estate investors. Whether you own rental properties in Billings, vacation rentals near Big Sky, or commercial buildings in Bozeman or Great Falls, a cost segregation study can dramatically accelerate your depreciation deductions. Under current bonus depreciation rules, many property owners recover a significant portion of their investment in the first year of ownership.

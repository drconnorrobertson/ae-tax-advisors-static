Strategic Tax Planning for Baltimore and Central Maryland

Maryland residents and business owners face a uniquely layered tax environment. Between federal obligations, Maryland state income tax, and local county or city income taxes, the total effective rate for high earners in the Baltimore area can climb significantly. Without proactive planning, many Baltimore professionals and entrepreneurs pay far more than they should.

At AE Tax Advisors, we provide comprehensive tax planning services from our Nottingham, MD office at 8843 Belair Rd, serving clients throughout Baltimore, Towson, Hunt Valley, Perry Hall, Bel Air, Columbia, Ellicott City, and the broader Baltimore County and Harford County region. Our team, led by Christina Nortman, works year-round to build customized strategies that reduce your tax liability, improve cash flow, and keep more of your income where it belongs.

Why Tax Planning Matters in Maryland

Maryland imposes a progressive state income tax with rates ranging from 2% to 5.75%, and most counties add a local income tax on top. Baltimore County, Baltimore City, Harford County, and Howard County each set their own local rates. For high-income business owners, business owners, and dual-income households across the Baltimore metro, the combined burden of federal, state, and local taxes demands a deliberate, forward-looking strategy.

Reactive tax preparation, filing after the year ends with no advance planning, leaves deductions and credits on the table. Our approach is different. We engage clients early and often, identifying opportunities throughout the year so you can take action while there is still time to make adjustments.

Tax Planning for Baltimore Business Owners

If you own a business in Baltimore or anywhere in Maryland, entity selection and tax structuring are among the most impactful decisions you will make. We help business owners evaluate S-Corp vs. LLC structures, determine reasonable compensation strategies, optimize retirement plan contributions, and time income and deductions for maximum benefit. Whether you operate a professional practice in Towson, a contracting firm in Perry Hall, or a tech startup in Columbia, your tax plan should reflect the specific dynamics of your business.

Our business owner tax planning services address the full range of considerations, including payroll tax optimization, Section 199A qualified business income deductions, and equipment expensing under Section 179. We also coordinate with your bookkeeper or CPA to ensure your financials support the strategies we implement.

Advanced Income & Entity Planning in the Baltimore Metro

Baltimore is home to major medical systems, defense contractors, financial institutions, and technology employers. Many professionals earning $200,000 or more in W-2 income assume there is little they can do to reduce their tax bill. That is not the case. Through strategies such as deferred compensation and equity planning, maximizing pre-tax retirement contributions, leveraging health savings accounts, and coordinating charitable giving, high earners in Maryland can significantly reduce their effective tax rate.

Our high-income business tax planning services are designed for professionals in Hunt Valley, Ellicott City, Bel Air, and across the Baltimore region who want a tax strategy as sophisticated as their career.

Quarterly and Year-End Tax Planning

Effective tax planning is not a once-a-year event. We work with clients on a quarterly cadence, reviewing income projections, estimated tax payments, and changes in financial circumstances. As year-end approaches, we conduct a detailed review to identify last-minute opportunities, including retirement plan contributions, asset purchases, charitable contributions, and income deferral or acceleration strategies.

This proactive approach ensures that you are never caught off guard by a large tax bill and that you are positioned to take advantage of every legitimate strategy available under the tax code.

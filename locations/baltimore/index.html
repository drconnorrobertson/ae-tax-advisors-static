Cost Segregation in Maryland

Maryland's real estate market presents exceptional opportunities for tax-savvy investors, and cost segregation is one of the most powerful tools available. A cost segregation study allows commercial and residential investment property owners to reclassify building components into shorter depreciation schedules of 5, 7, and 15 years instead of the standard 27.5 or 39 years. The result is dramatically accelerated depreciation deductions that put real cash back in your pocket.

Our cost segregation services in Maryland are especially valuable for investors in Baltimore County, Harford County, Howard County, and Anne Arundel County, where property values and acquisition costs can support significant reclassification benefits. Whether you own a multifamily property in Perry Hall, a mixed-use building in Towson, a warehouse in White Marsh, or a portfolio of rental homes in Bel Air, our engineering-based studies identify every eligible component and deliver IRS-compliant reports that stand up to scrutiny.

We have helped Maryland real estate investors unlock six-figure tax savings through cost segregation alone. Combined with strategies like bonus depreciation, Section 179 expensing, and the short-term rental tax loophole, the savings can be transformative for your bottom line.

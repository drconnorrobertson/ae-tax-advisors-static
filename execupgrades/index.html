What's Included in Your Tax Return Service

Professional Tax Return Preparation

Our expert CPAs and enrolled agents will prepare your federal and state tax returns with meticulous attention to detail, ensuring accuracy and maximizing deductions available to you.

Dedicated Tax Preparer

You'll work with the same dedicated tax professional throughout the entire process. They'll understand your unique situation and ensure continuity and personal attention.

Onboarding Call

We'll schedule a comprehensive onboarding call to collect all necessary information about your income, deductions, and tax situation. This ensures we capture everything needed for accurate preparation.

Secure Document Upload Portal

Access our encrypted document portal to securely upload all your tax documents—W-2s, 1099s, K-1s, investment statements, and more. Everything stays safe and organized.

E-Filing & Delivery

Once your return is finalized, we'll handle the e-filing with the IRS and relevant state agencies. You'll receive a fully prepared return ready for signature and submission.

Review & Sign

We'll walk you through your complete tax return, explaining all the details so you understand exactly what we're filing on your behalf. Peace of mind included.
